Privacy Policy for Mbot-discord

Last updated: June 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Mbot-discord handles your data when used in your Discord server.

  • Data Collected: The bot may temporarily process server and user IDs, as well as command input, to function properly. No messages or personal data are stored.
  • Use of Data: Any data processed is used exclusively to provide responses to commands and is not shared with third parties.
  • Data Storage: Mbot-discord does not store any data after execution. No databases or logs with identifiable user information are maintained.
  • Your Rights: You can remove the bot from your server at any time. If you have questions or concerns, feel free to contact the developer via GitHub Issues or Discord.
